Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is the glory and pride of India. Hundreds and thousands of visitors from India and overseas countries visit to witness one of the world’s seven wonders every day. This ivory-white marble mausoleum is explored by infinite tourists every day to see the alluring beauty and charisma this place holds. This has always been one of India’s many iconic places to explore and is surely on everyone’s bucket list. When in Agra, travelling to the Taj Mahal is a must. But you might need to put your plan to visit here on pause as this monument will be staying closed for some time.
